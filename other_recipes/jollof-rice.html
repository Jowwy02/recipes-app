Jollof Rice

Descripton

Jollof rice is a rice dish from West Africa. The dish is typically made with long grain rice, tomatoes, onions, spices, vegetables and meat in a single pot, although it's preparation method vary across differnt regions.
According to Wikipedia, Jollof derives it's name from the name of Wolof people.
This Jollof rice recipe is the best you will find on the internet.
It is a detailed step-by-step process that simplifies the cooking process so that anyone can easily cook it to perfection.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Bell Peppers
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Vegetable oil
  • Fresh ginger
  • Fresh garlic
  • Curry powder
  • Dried Thyme
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Bay leaves(optional)
  • Chicken broth
  • Salt

Steps to make Jollof rice

  1. Blend the bell peppers, tomatoes, onion, fresh ginger and fresh garlic. Place a pot heat, add oil into the pot, then add in sliced onions.
    Sautee the onions till they are fragrant and translucent. This should take about 2 minutes.
  2. Next, add in the tomato paste. Fry for 5-10 till reduced in quantity and doesn't taste tart.
  3. Add in the blended peppers, curry, thyme, seasoning cubes, bay leaves, giner, garlic and salt. Fry till it is dark red and no longer raw. It will be reduced in quantity, thick and the oil floats to the top of the sauce. This takes about 10-15 minutes.
  4. Then add in the chicken broth, and boiling water. Stir, add in the washed rice. Cover the pot tightly and cook the rice on low heat till it's almost tender. Should take about 25 minutes
  5. Add the remaining sliced onion, sliced tomato. Cover the pot and continue to steam the rice till it's tender.
  6. Stir the rice and take it off the heat.
    Serve and enjoy.
