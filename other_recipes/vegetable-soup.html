Vegetable Soup

Descripton

Vegetable soup is a traditional Nigerian soup that is highly nutritious and protein-packed. This finger-licking delicacy is also commonly known as Edikang Ikong Soup.
Edikang Ikong is a soup that is native to the Efik people from the Cross River, and Akwa Ibom states in Nigeria. This delicious soup tastes so good and has a load of health benefits.

Ingredients

  • Vegetables (Ugwu)
  • Waterleaf
  • Grounded Pepper
  • Onion
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Crayfish
  • Goat meat
  • Dried fish
  • Palm oil
  • Goat meat broth
  • Salt

Steps to make Vegetable-soup

Prepare the Goat Meat

  1. First, wash and cut the goat meat into small pieces or your preferred size (if not already cut).
  2. Put it into a pot and pour just enough water to cover it.
  3. Add the chopped onions, bouillon or Maggi cubes, salt, and pepper.
  4. Boil till soft (about 40-45 minutes should be enough
  5. Take out the meat and leave the stock in the pot for the soup.
