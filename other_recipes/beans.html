Porridge Beans

Description

Nigerian beans porridge, also known as stewed beans, is a dietary staple in many households in the country.
It is called “ewa riro” in Yoruba or sometimes just referred to as “ewa”, which means beans.
It is easily accessible to everyone and can be cooked without any fuss or a lot of added ingredients. It is a popular meal with students as it is a cheap way to feed a lot of people at once. Beans porridge can be prepared in different ways, usually as hearty, no fuss, “one pot” meal, but this recipe takes it from casual to extraordinary.

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Grounded peppers
  • Palm Oil
  • Onion
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Dried Fish(optional)
  • Salt
